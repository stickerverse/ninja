The Node is a new food truck located at 3240 SE Hawthorne Blvd and offers breakfast tacos and burritos (we recommend the Chorizo!), sandwiches, and waffles.

Available on Postmates as well!

They ordered a hybrid decal, meaning it was printed first then weeded.
Did you know we print with a large format printer and can do stickers up to 51″ wide? Let’s get crazy!

We meticulously measure to ensure everything is level before placing the decal.

Decals are harder to install during the winter season. The vinyl performs much better in warmer temperatures.
Heat guns come in handy on those colder days!

Signage for all sides!

Peeling off the masking tape is the most satisfying part of the job. Fresh decal!
