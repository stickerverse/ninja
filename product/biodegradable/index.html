⚠️⚠️ If you are wanting a sticker that is long lasting/doesn’t rub off/doesn’t fade in the sun, these are not those!! Biodegradable stickers will breakdown over time and are really only intended for certain use. This is not going to be a super durable sticker, that will last for years. But! It is a biodegradable sticker that will breakdown in compost within 12 weeks.
For reference: these will not last on a water bottle at all. ⚠️⚠️

Deets
  • – Made from a sugarcane byproduct
    – Printed with certified compostable & vegan inks
    – Scratches resistant
    – Splash Proof (but cannot be used for shower products)
    – Medium-strength adhesive, will stick to most surfaces
    – Can write on it!
    – Matte finishSimply put them in your food waste bin, and they will compost within 12 weeks ♻️
