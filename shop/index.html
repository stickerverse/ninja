Vinyl Types

Your artwork will be printed on one of the six specialty vinyls we offer in the Shiny section. We're pretty talented on knowing which vinyl would pair best with your artwork! The proof will reflect which material type you will be getting 💕 If there's a vinyl type you would prefer not to have used, please mention in the Print Notes!

Please note that when choosing this option, that while technically we can switch to a different material type during the Proofing process, keep in mind that we are humans who do each and every white ink layer by hand! Switching to a different material type means we will have to re-do the white ink layer to accommodate a different material type. We're a small business so please stay mindful when selecting this option 🙂

Color Morph (Cold)

Available Cuts

Die Cut

Notes

We out here getting involved in actual witchcraft for this color morph vinyl 😌 When this material gets below 59 degrees F, it changes to purple 💜

 

Great for non-insulated water bottles!

 

Comes matte laminated.

White Ink Layer

This magical material will slightly alter the colors in your artwork! Especially when it transforms from pink to purple.
This material type comes with a white ink layer which will allow the color morphing vinyl to show in select spots. As always, we're happy to design a white ink layer for you!

Artwork tips 🎨
Head's up! Black line artwork does not look great on this material, it gets lost on the purple morph. We can easily change your lines to white ink though, and will do that by default 🙂
Uploading artwork that is on a transparent PNG helps us out so much!!

Approximate Hex Codes
Pink: FF13B3
Purple: 7028BC

Biodegradable

Available Cuts

Die cut
Kiss Cut

Please read!!!!!!!!!!!

⚠️⚠️ If you are wanting a sticker that is long lasting/doesn't rub off/doesn't fade in the sun, these are not those!! Biodegradable stickers will breakdown over time and are really only intended for certain use. This is not going to be a super durable sticker, that will last for years. But! It is a biodegradable sticker that will compost within 12 weeks.
For reference: these will not last on a water bottle at all. ⚠️⚠️

Deets
  • – Made from a sugarcane byproduct
    – Printed with certified compostable & vegan inks
    – Scratches resistant
    – Splash Proof (but cannot be used for shower products)
    – Medium-strength adhesive, will stick to most surfaces
    – Can write on it!
    – Matte finishSimply put them in your food waste bin, and they will compost within 12 weeks ♻️
